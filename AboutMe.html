My start to programming


I first began programming in my junior year of high school back in 2018. My first language I learned was Swift, which is how Apple makes their apps.
It was hard at first as I have never coded before, but Swift is more of a layed back language, so learning the basics of coding with that language was nice.
After a semester, I took the class again, but I was allowed to develop potential apps for the school. I was able to fully publish one app for the school.

Final Grade Calculator


With this application, students were able to enter current grades into textboxes and then the app would tell them what grade they would need on the final to pass.
