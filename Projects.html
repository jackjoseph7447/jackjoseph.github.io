Final Grade Calculator


With this application, students were able to enter current grades into textboxes and then the app would tell them what grade they would need on the final to pass. The first image (The image on the left) is the main screen of the application. It is a very basic layout, but this app was just to get something out there for the students to use, it was not really meant to have a complex system. The second image (The image on the right) displays a quick tutorial about how to use the app and what everything means. This is displayed by pressing the question mark button in the top left of the screen. If you would like to view the repository for the project, click here.
